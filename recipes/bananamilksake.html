Banana Milkshake

Description

An easy banana shake you can make in minutes, with creamy vanilla ice cream, a pinch of cinnamon and a swizzle of whipped cream

Banana milkshake recipe that is the absolute best ever! Milk, vanilla ice cream, one banana, and lemon juice make this so creamy and thick!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups (about 3 scoops) vanilla ice cream
  • 1/2 to 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 large banana
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla
  • Cinnamon sugar
  • Whipped cream (optional)

Steps

  1. In your blender, combine slightly softened ice cream (for easier blending), a banana broken into pieces, and milk. Blend until smooth and pourable.
  2. Pour into a glass and add whipped cream and a sprinkle of cinnamon sugar if desired. And enjoy!
